FINAL PROJECT

Flicker Shadow
Disappearance

2025

About

This project is an artist book I made from a candle performance. I used light, shadow, mirrors, and candles shaped like a body. The book is about memory, disappearance, and what is left behind.

Medium Artist book, photography, performance

Format 16-page booklet

Tools InDesign and photography

“The body holds its light until it learns how to disappear.”

Process

I arranged candles on the floor to create a body shape, then photographed different moments as the light changed. I later organized the images into a small booklet with short captions.
