project

Flicker Shadow Disappearance

Flicker Shadow Disappearance is an artist book I made from a candle performance. I arranged red candles on the floor in the shape of a body and photographed the light, shadows, mirror reflections, and moments of disappearance.

The project is about memory, the body, and what remains after something is gone. I wanted the book to feel quiet and slow, almost like the viewer is watching the body appear and disappear through light.

process

I started by setting up candles on a concrete floor. I used a mirror, darkness, and changing light to create different images. After taking the photographs, I selected the strongest moments and placed them into a small booklet with short poetic captions.
