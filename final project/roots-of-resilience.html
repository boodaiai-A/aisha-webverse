public art proposal

Roots of Resilience

Roots of Resilience is a public art proposal about protection, growth, and the connection between people and the natural environment. The sculpture uses leaf and root forms to show strength, movement, and care for nature.

I wanted the piece to feel organic and protective, like something growing from the ground while also holding space for memory and resilience.
