MY PASSION: DOGS (SIMBA)

This page is about something I love. I will update it during the semester.

Why I love dogs

I didn’t always understand how strong the bond with a dog can be until Simba became part of my life. He has a calm personality but also a playful side that surprises me every day. Sometimes when I feel stressed from school, he just sits next to me quietly and that alone helps a lot.

  • He makes normal days feel lighter.
  • Walking him helps me slow down and notice small things.
  • He reminds me to be patient and present.
Some days Simba feels like a quiet shadow. Not loud, not demanding — just present.

I chose dogs as my passion because they represent loyalty and emotional connection. Simba is not just a pet to me — he feels like a quiet companion that understands without words.
