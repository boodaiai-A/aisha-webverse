MY PASSION: DOGS (SIMBA)

This page is about something I love. I will update it during the semester.

Why I love dogs

  • Simba makes me feel calm when I’m stressed.
  • Taking care of him taught me responsibility and patience.
  • Dogs give real love and comfort every day.

Simba is small, but his personality is big. When I’m studying late, he stays close. Even when the day is hard, he makes the house feel warmer.

Little things Simba does that I notice
  • He follows me from room to room like a shadow.
  • He gets excited when he hears keys or a snack bag.
  • He sits next to me when I’m quiet or tired.
